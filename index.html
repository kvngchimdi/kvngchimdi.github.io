About Us

Our Story

Our Story Founded in 2023 in the heart of Flic en Flac, Mauritius, Hich Lounge began with a vision to create a vibrant dining experience celebrating bold flavors. From our signature burgers and sizzling kebabs to artisanal pizzas and exotic dishes, we use premium ingredients and time-honored recipes to craft every plate with passion. Today, Hich Lounge remains a beloved local gem, inviting guests to savor unforgettable meals in a warm, lively atmosphere

The Owner's Journey

Welcome to our family restaurant, where we invite you on a delicious journey to the world of Iranian flavors. Since June 2023, we proudly bring a variety of Iranian cuisines to Mauritius, a country in South Africa. From kebabs and flavorful stews to delightful local dishes, at our restaurant, you can enjoy a diverse and unforgettable taste of Iran. Join us and experience a culinary journey filled with Iranian gastronomy
