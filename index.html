About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Maravann Burgers started as a small food truck passionate about crafting perfect burgers. Our secret recipes and premium ingredients quickly made us a local favorite. Today, we continue to serve mouthwatering burgers with the same love and dedication.

The Owner's Journey

The owner's dream begins with to create unforgettable burger experiences. Starting from humble beginnings, he has poured his heart into mastering the art of burger crafting, transforming his restaurant into a beloved location in the heart of Mauritius.
